Taj Mahal

Let's Know about Taj Mahal

  • Wonder's Name: Taj Mahal
  • Location: India
  • Material:White marble
  • Place and Others:The Taj Mahal, a world-renowned mausoleum in Agra, India, was commissioned by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Known for its architectural beauty and symbol of eternal love, its intricate carvings, stunning symmetry, and beautiful gardens make it one of the most admired monuments globally.

Features of Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal- Symbol Of Love

  • Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal in 1653
  • The Taj Mahal is often called the Epitiom of Love

Taj Mahal- Marble Masterpiece

  • Constructed with white marble that changes the color with the light
  • The Taj Mahal looks different at different times of the day

Taj Mahal- Architectural Wonder

  • Its perfect symmetry, including the iconic central dome and four minarets, is a marvel of Mughal architecture
